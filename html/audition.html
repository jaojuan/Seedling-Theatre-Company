OUR STAGE IS READY FOR YOU

ACT, SING, PERFORM

Welcome to Seedling Theatre Company! We are a performing arts studio servicing the Henderson, Southern Las Vegas and Boulder City areas. We offer a variety of courses for kids ages 3 to 18 years. Our studio has a fully equipped theatre (stage, lighting, sound) as well as additional rooms for dance and vocal performance classes. Please join our email list for regular updates by filling out the contact form below.

JRS SHOWCASE CAST MUSICAL THEARE: SEUSSICAL THE MUSICAL JR
No registration for this class is available at this time. IF YOU ARE INTERESTED IN AUDITIONING FOR A SHOWCASE CLASS, IT IS HIGHLY RECOMMENDED THAT YOU ENROLL NOW IN A "BACKUP" NON-SHOWCASE Seussical CLASS. If you are cast after auditions, any payments for the back-up class will be transferred to your Showcase class.

Enrollments in SHOWCASE CAST musical theatre classes are on an AUDITION-ONLY basis. Showcase Casts are advanced level performance classes. Aside from an in-person vocal and acting audition, attendance, behavior in previous classes, adherence to the Seedling Code of Conduct (including phone use in class), level of dedication and commitment, focus, and respect for instructors and fellow classmates are all taken into consideration when considering students for Showcase classes.

If there are other commitments on Tuesday evenings that you know will prevent consistent attendance to this Showcase class, please consider a class on another night.

Auditions will be held on Dec 4th, 2021 at the Seedling Theatre Company studio. If you are interested in auditioning, please fill out the contact form at the bottom of any page on the website to be sent audition information and materials. Early auditions can be arranged if you cannot make this date. Showcase cast will receive their scripts after casts are set and are expected to be OFF-BOOK BY THE FIRST DAY OF CLASSES.
