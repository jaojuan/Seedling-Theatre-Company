OUR STAGE IS READY FOR YOU

ACT, SING, PERFORM

Welcome to Seedling Theatre Company! We are a performing arts studio servicing the Henderson, Southern Las Vegas and Boulder City areas. We offer a variety of courses for kids ages 3 to 18 years. Our studio has a fully equipped theatre (stage, lighting, sound) as well as additional rooms for dance and vocal performance classes. Please join our email list for regular updates by filling out the contact form below.

Ms Kris Blake

Seedling’s slogan, “Where young performers are grown,” speaks to Blake’s experience with directing kids amid raising her three children. Blake earned a degree in youth leadership at Brigham Young University before pursuing a master’s degree in recreation management, which she said she wasn’t able to finish under the demands of motherhood. After working at Henderson drama school Hollywood Kids Academy for three years, then directing plays at her daughter’s school, American Heritage Academy, for a year, Blake was ready to pave her own way to success.

Our Mission

Seedling Theatre Company is dedicated to providing an environment
where children and teens feel welcome, encouraged, and empowered
through participating in the performing arts and where instructors teach
with patience and love, striving to develop their potential in every student.

OUR COMPANY IS...

child centered

"Whether your child is just starting to break out of their shell or headed for Broadway tomorrow, we want to provide as many challenges and opportunities for advancement as possible."

learning based

"We believe that every student, in every class, of every day we are open should be learning something new."

family oriented

"Throughout the year we will have many opportunities for families to get involved in special performances and events."

community involved

"We are so proud of our students, past and present, for the contributions they are making to their schools and community organizations through theatre, choir, orchestra, dance, and student government."
